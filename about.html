About Us

Ayadi Glocal School is an online CBSE school designed for children of expatriates in the GCC and around the world. It offers a flexible, personalized learning experience with subjects beyond the standard curriculum, including French, Spanish, Robotics, and Coding. The school emphasizes personal mentoring and affordable fees without compromising on quality.

Online CBSE Education:
Ayadi Glocal School provides a complete online CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education) curriculum from KG to 8th grade.

Subjects Beyond Textbooks:
In addition to core subjects, they offer subjects like French, Spanish, Robotics, and Coding, aiming to prepare students for a technology-driven world.

Personal Mentoring:
A key feature of Ayadi Glocal School is its focus on personal mentoring, ensuring each student receives tailored educational planning.

Affordable and Flexible:
The school aims to offer quality education at affordable fees and provides flexible online classes, allowing students to learn from anywhere.

Focus on Global Citizenship:
They aim to equip students to become global citizens, emphasizing multilingualism and a strong foundation for the future.

Based in India:
The school is an Indian LLP (Limited Liability Partnership) registered in Kozhikode, Kerala.

Target Audience:
The school primarily caters to expatriate families in the Gulf region, but also serves students globally.
